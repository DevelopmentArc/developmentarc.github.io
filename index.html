Guiding is our passion...

DevelopmentArc® was founded in 2008 to provide software development, management and technology services for a wide range of companies. Our goal is to help you and your team define goals, provide a process to implement them and then guarantee that you retain ownership of your technology investments.

Over the years, we have worked with a wide range of companies: from Fortune 500 organizations to stealth-mode start-ups. Every company and team has their own needs and challenges and at DevelopmentArc® we take the time to understand this and apply our experience to achieve your vision.

We do this by taking the time analyze and understand your current position before making any recommendations. Under planning can be just as dangerous as over-planning and we strive to find the perfect balance for everyone involved. Once we have a solid grasp of your needs we make recommendations on how to approach the solution. This involves technology recommendations, architectural oversight, development & cost estimations, team additions (if required), technology implementation and infrastructure & system investments to achieve your end goals.

Services for the web and beyond...

At DevelopmentArc® we provide a variety of services for our clients. Our main focus is web-enabled technologies, from desktop to devices, from front-end UI to back-end deployments.

  • Project analysis and cost estimation
  • Application Architecture and system planning
  • Full Stack development (UI to Back-end services)
  • Team expansion & development
  • Customized in-depth training on a variety of web technologies
  • We specialize in:
    • JavaScript based applications (React, jQuery, ES6, etc.)
    • Ruby on Rails (server development)
    • JS Build Systems (Gulp, NPM, Webpack)
    • React Native & Apache Cordova (cross-device application development)
    • Postgres, MongoDB & MySQL
    • Redis, Memcache & ElasticSearch
    • Heroku & Amazon Web Services (AWS)
